Discover Bio-Agents: The Last Hope

Due to constant wars, mankind was almost wiped out from existence. The entire atmosphere of the Earth was left in uninhabitable conditions. In their last effort, what was left of humanity created the Bio-Agents, organisms capable of surviving Earth's toxic atmosphere.

The objective of the game is to purify the zones by defeating mutant Beasts categorized as "Primordial", which are the ones that deliver toxins to the atmosphere, affecting all the flora and fauna, giving them more pawns in their mutant armies.
